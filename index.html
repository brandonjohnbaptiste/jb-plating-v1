Welcome to our silver antique repair service page!

At JB Plating Co, we understand the importance of preserving the beauty and history of your silver antiques. We specialize in repairing and restoring all kinds of silver antiques, including tea sets, flatware, jewellery, bowls, trophies, candlesticks, trays and more.

Our team of expert craftsmen have years of experience in repairing and restoring silver antiques, and we use only the highest quality materials and techniques to ensure that your silver treasures are restored to their original glory.

We take pride in our attention to detail and our commitment to preserving the integrity of your antique pieces.
